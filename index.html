Rules

The computer selects four colours out of the six displayed on the colour board and creates a code. The player then needs to guess the code by selecting a colour from the colour board and placing it in one of the board holes. Once the player is happy with their colour selection, press the submit button to submit your guess. The computer will then give you some clues: A red peg indicates the correct colour in the correct position. A white peg indicates a correct colour in the wrong position. The player will use this feedback to make subsequent guesses, attempting to deduce the correct colours and their positions. The game continues until the player guesses the correct code or runs out of attempts (10 rows). The player wins if they guess the correct code within 10 attempts. The computer wins if the player fails to guess the code within 10 attempts.
